I am Deepthi Devaki Akkoorath
and I am a PhD student in the Software Technology Group at TU Kaiserslautern, supervised by Dr. rer. nat. Annette Bieniusa and Prof. Dr. Arnd Poetzsch-Heffter.

My research is on weak memory consistency in concurrent and distributed systems. Currently I am working on Antidote: a scalable key-value store for synchronisation free computations using CRDTs and causal consistent transactions. I am also interested in the semantics and applicability of weak/eventual consistency in the context of shared memory concurrent programs and Software Transactional Memory (STM).

I have workd priviously as Software Developer at MulticoreWare for x265 HEVC development.
